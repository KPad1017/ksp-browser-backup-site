1. Overview

KSP Browser Backup is a Windows application developed by KSPadgett. It is designed to back up and restore local browser profiles on your device. The application does not require an online account or connection, except when you voluntarily store backups on a network path you control.

2. Information the App Accesses

To perform backups and restores, the app accesses browser profile folders stored in your Windows user account. Depending on the browser, this may include:

  • Bookmarks and favorites
  • Extensions and extension data
  • Preferences, configurations, and settings
  • Cookies and session data
  • Encrypted password databases (browser-managed)
  • Profile metadata and user interface settings

All accessed data remains on your device or on a storage location you choose (local drive, external drive, NAS, or network directory).

3. What the App Collects or Sends

The app does NOT collect, transmit, upload, or share any personal data. Specifically:

  • No telemetry or analytics are collected.
  • No browsing data leaves your device.
  • No profile information is transmitted to KSPadgett, Microsoft, or third parties.
  • No cloud services are used.
  • No login, account, or internet access is required.

All file operations occur locally and only with the storage paths you select.

4. Local Storage, Backups, and Encryption

The app allows you to choose any storage path for backups. Optionally, you may enable encryption:

  • Encrypted archives use AES-based protection within ZIP/7z containers.
  • You choose the encryption password.
  • Your password is never transmitted or sent outside your PC.
  • If you choose to save the password using Windows Credential Manager, it is stored securely through Windows.
  • KSPadgett cannot recover or reset lost encryption passwords.

5. Logs and Diagnostics

The app may create local log files to help you review backup and restore activity. These logs:

  • Remain entirely on your device
  • Do not contain browsing content or your saved passwords
  • Can be deleted at any time

6. Children’s Privacy

The software is designed for general use and does not target children or collect information from them.

7. Data Retention

Since the app does not transmit or store data externally, all retention is controlled by you. Backups remain wherever you place them until you delete them.

8. Compliance and Legal Basis

This app follows Microsoft Store data-handling rules and general privacy principles. Because all data remains on your system, any access, correction, or deletion rights are managed directly by you.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. When updated, we will revise the “Last Updated” date. Continued use of the application indicates acceptance of the updated Policy.
